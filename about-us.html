Welcome to CELESTE'25

CELESTE'25 is our college’s (KITS-KOTTAYAM INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY AND SCIENCE) premier TECH Fest, designed to celebrate creativity, innovation, and the spirit of discovery in TECHNOLOGY. Organized by passionate final-year students (XENOS- BATCH 2021-2025), our fest brings together tech enthusiasts from across campus and beyond to explore emerging technologies, share ideas, and ignite new possibilities.

Our Story

We want to implement our ideas into a dynamic, multi-day event. Inspired by our desire to create a platform for innovation, we set out to build an event that not only showcases our ideas but also fosters collaboration and learning from it. Over the years, Tech-Fest has grown into a vibrant program where every participant can contribute, learn, and be inspired.

Mission & Vision

  • Mission: To create an engaging environment that empowers students to explore, innovate, and push the boundaries of technology.

  • Vision: To become a catalyst for technological advancement, making tech ideas accessible and exciting for everyone, and inspiring the next generation of tech students.

Meet the Team

We are a dedicated group of final-year students (XENOS), each bringing unique skills to the table—from event planning and marketing to tech support and creative ideas. Our team works tirelessly behind the scenes to ensure every aspect of CELESTE'25 is nothing short of exceptional.

We would also like to extend our deepest gratitude to our supportive staff, professors, and coordinators. Their invaluable guidance, mentorship, and encouragement have been instrumental in bringing this event to life. We couldn't have done it without their support!

What to Expect?

At CELESTE'25, you’ll experience a mix of engaging fun competitions designed to spark your creativity and fuel your passion for technology. Whether you’re a seasoned coder or just curious about the tech world, there’s something here for everyone.
