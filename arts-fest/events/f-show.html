FASHION SHOW

Rules and Regulations

  • TEAM SIZE:3-8 MEMBERS (Minimum of 3 and maximum of 8 members).
  • Walking on barefoot is not allowed.
  • TIME LIMIT: 6 minutes. Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
  • No gender restrictions on team constitution.
  • Any form of obscenity or vulgarity leads to direct disqualification.
  • Participants are to mandatorily bring their songs and submit it to the coordinators prior to the performance.
  • Use of cigarettes, alcohol and any unfair means as property is strongly prohibited.
  • Teams will be judged on costumes, theme, walking stance and attitude.
  • The group should decide their own theme and perform accordingly.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
