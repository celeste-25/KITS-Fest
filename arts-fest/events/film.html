FILM SCENE RECREATION

Rules and Regulations

  • Recreate any of your favourite movie/web series/tv show scenes with the resources you have.
  • The performance must be staged live. Ensure your setup is ready for a seamless live act.
  • The performance can be up to 8 minutes long, excluding the introduction and credits.
  • There is no language barrier. However, a brief summary of the scene should be provided before the performance if the scenes are in languages other than Telugu, Hindi, and English.
  • You can use ideas or scenes that have been previously performed.
  • You may include background images, GIFs, or videos related to your scenes. These clips must be submitted to the coordinators at least 1 day before the event and should be in 16:9(landscape) resolution.
