FILM SCENE RECREATION

Rules and Regulations

  • Recreate any of your favourite movie/web series/tv show scenes with the resources you have.
  • The videos must be in LANDSCAPE mode only. (16:9)
  • The videos can be 1 to 4 minutes long, excluding the credits.
  • There is no language barrier. But subtitles should be provided if the scenes are in languages other than Telugu, Hindi, and English.
  • You can submit previously recorded videos but are not allowed to submit content previously submitted to any past editions of this competition.
