Group Dance

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team may have 3-8 members including coordinators.
  • Time limit is maximum 8 minutes for each performance.
  • No props will be provided by the coordinators, participants can carry their own props.
  • The music should be provided in a pen drive prior to the event.
  • Participants are not allowed to mix dialogs within the performance.
  • Judgement will be based on choreography, rhythms, synchronization, costume, makeup and overall presentation and performances.
  • Green rooms would be given for changing purpose.
  • Dance performances should not convey any indecent gestures.
  • Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
