Group Song

Rules and Regulations

  • The maximum time limit allowed is 5 minutes, if not be disqualified.
  • Each team may have 2-6 members including coordinators
  • Participants can choose any song of their choice irrespective of the language for the prelims
  • Karoke is permitted
  • Participants will not be allowed to refer to the lyrics while singing
  • Classical songs are not allowed
  • It is mandatory to submit the Karoke at least one hour the before the performance on a mobile phone or a pen drive
  • The judging will be based on voice quality, clarity rhythm and their appeal to the audience
  • The decision of the judges will be final
  • Online registrations are allowed and no spot registration.
