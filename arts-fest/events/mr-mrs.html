Mr. & Mrs.

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team should consist of 2 members of a boy and girl.
  • Participants should do a performance together.
  • There will be a questionnaire from the judges after the performance.
  • Participants should inform their names and details before the event, no spot registration is allowed.
  • Judgement will be based on synchronisation, choreography, expression.
  • The decision of judges will be final.
  • If these rules are not followed the participants will be disqualified.
