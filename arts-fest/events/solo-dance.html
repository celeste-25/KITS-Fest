Solo Dance

Rules and Regulations

  • The maximum time limit allowed is 4 minutes, if not be disqualified.
  • Freestyle, cinematic, hip pop, semi-classical.
  • Pure Classical Dance is not allowed.
  • It is mandatory to submit the song at least one hour the before the performance on a mobilephone or a pen drive.
  • judgement will be based on choreography, rhythms, synchronization, costume, makeup and overall presentation and performances.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • Online registrations are allowed and No spot registration.
