Womens - Throwball

Rules and Regulations

  • Each team consists of 7 players on the court at a time. (5 substitution)
  • Starting Play: A coin toss decides which team serves first. The game starts with the serving team throwing the ball into the opponent's court.
  • Scoring: Points are scored when the ball lands in the opponent's court, or if the opponent commits a fault (like holding the ball).
  • Winning: The team first to reach 15 points (with a minimum 2-point lead) wins a set. The match is typically played best of 3 sets.
  • Participants are requested to report at least half an hour before each event at the venue.
  • Any person, if not ready by the time will be disqualified.
  • Any form of obscene, unacceptable behaviour or language, vulgarity, on or off stage will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • If these rules are not followed the participants will be disqualified.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding on all participants in all events.
  • Any complaints regarding judges or their judgement will not be entertained.
  • Organizers enjoy the right to admission, right to modify the rules and to change or cancel any.
