Face Painting

Rules and Regulations

  • Judgement will be based on the theme.
  • This is a team event of two participants in a team.
  • Please do not participate if you have sensitive skin or allergies, We will not be responsible for the skin reactions.
  • Participants should bring their own materials (color, brushes, etc...)
  • At least 30%-50% face should be covered.
  • Only use proffesional face paint and glitters that are approved for use on the face and eye area.
