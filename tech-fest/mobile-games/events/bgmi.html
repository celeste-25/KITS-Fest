BGMI - Battle Grounds Mobile India

Rules and Regulations

  • All players must join the match within the designated time.
  • Players must remain in the game until the match ends.
  • No use of third-party tools or hacks; fair play is required.
  • Respect your teammates and opponents during the game.
  • The player with the most kills or the last survivor wins.
  • In case of any disputes, the organizers' decision is final.
