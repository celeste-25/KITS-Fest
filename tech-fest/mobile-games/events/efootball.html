Rules and Regulations

  • Players must participate individually in the tournament.
  • Matches will follow a knockout format, with each match being 1v1.
  • Players should not use any unfair advantages like third-party tools.
  • Respect your opponent and follow the rules during gameplay.
  • The winner will be determined based on the match score.
  • Organizers' decision will be final in case of any disputes or conflicts.
