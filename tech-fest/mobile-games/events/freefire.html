Free Fire

Rules and Regulations

  • Players must enter the game within the allocated time frame.
  • Solo and team modes are available for participation.
  • Any kind of cheating or unfair play will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Maintain sportsmanship and respect for all players during the game.
  • The winner is the last survivor or the player/team with the most kills at the end.
  • Organizers' decisions are final in case of any disputes.
